Dear Tower Porch Hosts,

Welcome! Whether this is your first year opening your porch or you’ve been with us since the beginning, welcome to the 2026 Tower Porchfest landscape — and thank you for choosing to be part of it. Tower Porchfest 2026 will take place on Saturday, April 25, 10am-7pm. Because of hosts like you, this continues to be one of the very best days of the year in Fresno. Your willingness to share your space helps transform our neighborhood into a living, breathing celebration of music, art, and community.

As we finalize event materials, the Tower Porchfest Committee kindly asks that you update your performance lineup as soon as possible. Final edits will be accepted through March 29th, as performer names will be included on this year’s annual commemorative t-shirt. We don’t want anyone left off, so please be sure your information is accurate and complete before the deadline.

We’re also excited to invite you to our Tower Porchfest “Sound Check” Pre-Party on Sunday, April 12th from 12pm–4pm at Gazebo Gardens. This will be your opportunity to collect yard signs, host materials, and other giveaways — and to connect with fellow hosts before the big day.

A quick but important reminder: please be mindful of sound bleed throughout the event. To ensure that neighbors not participating in Porchfest can also enjoy their homes, we will be checking decibel readings periodically throughout the day. Keeping volume at reasonable levels helps maintain the positive community spirit that makes this event possible.
